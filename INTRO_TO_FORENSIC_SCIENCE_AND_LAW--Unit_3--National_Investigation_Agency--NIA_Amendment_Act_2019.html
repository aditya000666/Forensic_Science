NIA Amendment Act 2019


• Passed by parliament and received Presidential assent in July 2019
• Few changes to NIA Act 2008
• Allows agency to investigate on offences as
◇ Human trafficking
◇ Counterfeit currency or bank notes related offences
◇ Sale or manufacture of prohibited items
◇ Offences under Explosive Substance Act, 1908
◇ Cyber terrorism
• Expand jurisdiction of NIA
• NIA has authority to investigate offences committed outside Indian Territory subject to International treaties and domestic laws of other nations
• Allows Central Government to constitute Special Courts to conduct trials of scheduled offences
◇ Govt. designate Session courts as Special courts, after consulting Chief Justice of High Court
◇ Act authorises State government to designate Special Courts
◇ Government appoint more than Special court in area
◇ Currently, 38 NIA Courts across states and 7 across UT
◇ Special Govt. judges appointed by GOI in consultation with High Court Chief justice of area
◇ Trial of NIA Special courts have precedence over trial of accused in any other case
