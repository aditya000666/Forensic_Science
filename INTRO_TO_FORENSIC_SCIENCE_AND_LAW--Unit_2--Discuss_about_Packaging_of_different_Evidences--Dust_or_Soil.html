Dust or Soil


• Dust or Soil should be placed in filter paper and then enclosed in suitable container
• If dust found on any article of furniture, it can be collected directly in filter paper with help of vaccum cleaner
• If dust is found on object which can be readily transported such as shoe or clothing, the whole object should be sent to lab keeping dust or soil intact on material
• Metal filing, glass fragments, finger nail scraping, paint chips, placed in filter paper and enclosed in suitable containers
