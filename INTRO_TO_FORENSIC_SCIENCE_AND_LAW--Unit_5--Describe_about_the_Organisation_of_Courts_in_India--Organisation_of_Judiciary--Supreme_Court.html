Supreme Court


• Location: Delhi
• Highest judicial forum and final court of appeal
• Enjoys writ, appellate, original and advisory jurisdiction
• In addition to this, Supreme Court and High Courts also enjoy power of judicial review
• It is an appellate court which takes up appeals against judgments of High Courts of states and territories
• It also takes writ petition in cases of serious human rights violation or any petition filed under Article 32 which is right to consititutional remedies or if case involves serious issue that needs immediate resolution
• Supreme Court comprises the Chief Justice and 30 other judges
• Every judge of Suprement Court shall be appointed by President
• Supreme Court pass any punishment from simple imprisonment to death sentence
