Section 291 CrPC


Deposition of Medical Witness



• Deposition of civil surgeon or other medical witness, taken and attested by Magistrate in presence of accused or taken commission in this chapter, given evidence in injury, trial or other proceeding under this code, alothough deponent is not called witness
• Court may think if it fits and shall on applicatioin of prosecution or accused, summon and examine any such deponent as to subject matter of his deposition
