Section 66A


Punishment for Sending offensive messages through Communication Service



• Information that is grossly offensive or has menacing character
• Information which he knows to be false but for purpose of causing annoyane, inconvenience, danger, obstruction, insult, injury, criminal intimidation, enmity, hatred or ill will, persistently by making use of such computer resource
• Any electronic mail or E-mail message for purpose of causing annoyance or inconvenience or to deceive or to mislead the addressee or recepient about origin of message
• Shall be punished imprisonment for 3 yrs and with fine
