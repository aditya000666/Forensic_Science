Patent Protection


• Territorial right and therefore it is effective only within territory of india
• Filing an application in India enables the applicant to file corresponding application for same invention in convetion countries, within or before expiry of 12 months from filing date in India
• Separate patents should be obtained in each country where applicant requires protection of invention in those counties
• No patent is valid worldwide
• Possible to file an international application known as PCT application at Patent Office in Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, Chennai
• All these offices act as Receiving Office for International applications
