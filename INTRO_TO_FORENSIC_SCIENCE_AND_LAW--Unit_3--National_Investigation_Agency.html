National Investigation Agency (NIA)
- Central Counter-terrorism agency functioning under Ministry of Home Affairs, Govt. of India
- Agency empowered to deal with investigation of terror related crimes across states without special permission from states under written proclamation
- Investigates terror offences, waging ware against country, offences on nuclear facilities
- Came into force after National Investigation Agency Act 2008
- Created after Mumbai Terror Attack
- Headquarter: Delhi
- Agency aim is to become professional investigating agency matching global standards
- Aims at being detterent for existing and potential terrorists apart from being storhouse of information on terrorism
- NIA functions under Ministry of Home Affairs
- Maintains NIA “Most Wanted List”
