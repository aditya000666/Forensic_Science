Describe about Structure of Police


• According to Article 245 of Indian Constitution, Police is a sibject and its organisation and working are governed by rules and regulations framed by state government
• These rules and regulations are outlide in Police Manuals of state police forces
• Each State/Union Territory has its separate Police Force

• Structure of State Police Forces governed by Police Acy of 1861 applicable to most parts of country or by State Police Acts modeled mostly on 1861 legislation
• Major criminal laws, like Indian Penal Code, the Code of Criminal Procedure, Indian Evidence Act are uniformly applicable to almost all parts of country
• Indian Police Service is All Indian Service which is recruited, trained and managed by Central Government and which provides bulk of officers to State Police Forces
