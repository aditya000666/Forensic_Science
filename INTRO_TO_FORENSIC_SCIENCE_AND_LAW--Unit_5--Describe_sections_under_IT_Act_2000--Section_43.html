Section 43


Penalty and Compensation for Damage to Computer, Computer System etc



• Unauthorised access to computer
• Download, copies or extract any data
• Introduce or cause to be introduced any Computer virus
• Damage or causes to be damaged any computer
• Disrupt or Disruption of any computer
• Denies or causes Denial of access
• Charges the service availed of by person to account of another person
• Destroy, Delete or Alter any information residing in computer resource
• Steal, Conceal, Destroy or Alter or Cause any person to steal, conceal, destroy or alter
• Imprisonment for 3 yrs or fine of 5 lakh rupees
