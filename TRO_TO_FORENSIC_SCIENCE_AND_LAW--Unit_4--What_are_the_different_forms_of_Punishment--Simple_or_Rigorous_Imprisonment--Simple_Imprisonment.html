Simple Imprisonment


• Imposed for lighted offences
• Example
◇ Absconding to avoid service of summons or other proceeding
◇ Not attending in obedience to an order from public servant (Section 172-174)
◇ Obstruct traffic or cause Public Nuisance; Eve-Teasing
◇ Refusing oath when duly required to take oath by public servant (Section 178)
◇ Wrongful restraint (Section 341)
◇ Defamation (Sectio 500)
