What are the Objectives of Punishment


• Prevention of crime and every punishment is inteded to have double effect
• Prevent the person who has committed a crime from repeating the act
• Prevent other members of society from committing similar crimes
• Punishment is infliction of unpleasant or negative experience on offender in response to an offense
• In ancient times, the sole purpose of punishment was retribution
• In modern societies the objectives of punishment inlcude deterrence, retribution, incapacitation, rehabilitation and reparation
