Bailable Offence


• Offences or crimes that are not very serious in nature
• Police has the power to grand bail in these type of cases
• In such case bail is a right and arrested person must be released after depositing the bail
• Defendant may be able to secure his release upon payment of bail
• Example
◇ Simple Hurt (Section 337:IPC)
◇ Bribery (Section 171E:IPC)
◇ Public Nuisance (Section 290:IPC)
