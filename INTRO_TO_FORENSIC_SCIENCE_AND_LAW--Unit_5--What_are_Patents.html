What are Patents


• Statutory right for invention granted for limited period of time to patentee by Govt.
• Exchange of full disclosure of invention for excluding others from making money, using, selling, importing of patented product or process for producing that product for those purposes without his consent
• An invention relating either to product or process that is new, involving inventive step and capable of industrial acceptance can be patented
• Must not fall into cateogires of inventions that are non-patentable under Section 3 and 4
