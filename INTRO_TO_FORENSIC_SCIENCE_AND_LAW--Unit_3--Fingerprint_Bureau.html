FINGERPRINT BUREAU
• Idea fingerprint could be used as means of personal identification
• Put forward by Sir William Herschel in 1858
• First ever Fingerprint Bureau established at Writer's Building Calcutta in 1897
• CFPB is apex body in country which coordinate, guide, monitor and provide technical support to State Finger Print Bureau as well as Investigation Agencies and Internation organisation is all matters related to Finger Print Science
