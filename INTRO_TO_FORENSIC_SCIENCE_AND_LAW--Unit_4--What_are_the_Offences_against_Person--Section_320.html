Section 320


Grevious Hurt



1. Emasculation
2. Permanent privation of sight of eye
3. Permanent privation of hearing of ear
4. Privation of any member or joint
5. Destruction or premanent impairing of powers of any member or joint
6. Permanent disfiguration of head or face
7. Fracture or dislocation of bone or tooth
8. Any hurt which endangers life or which causes the sufferer to be during the space of 20 days in sever bodily pain or unable to follow ordinary pursuits
