Types of Photography


Purpose of Photographs



• Create permanent record of events occured at scene of crime
• Show accuracy of events without any bias
• Bring out certain facts to light which may not be possible to detect with naked eye
• Prove point of information or giving clues to those who decide the case based on photographic evidence
• To enable investigator to consult scientists, senior officiers, legal advisers about the case with help of photographs to arrive at correct location
