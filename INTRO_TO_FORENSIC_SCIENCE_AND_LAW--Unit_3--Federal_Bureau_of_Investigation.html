Federal Bureau of Investigation


• Principle investigative agency of federal government of United States
• Bureau is responsible for conducting investigations in cases where federal laws may have been violated, unless another agency of federal government has been specifically delegated that duty by statute or executive flat
• Part of Department of Justice, FBI reports results of investigations to attorney general of United States and his assistants in Washington and to United States attorney offices in country federal judicial districts
• Although being federal agency, FBI is not national police force and law enforcement in United States remains principally the responsibility of State and Local Governments
