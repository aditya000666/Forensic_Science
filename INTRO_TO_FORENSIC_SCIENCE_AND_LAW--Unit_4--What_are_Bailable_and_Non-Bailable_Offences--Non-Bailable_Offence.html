Non-Bailable Offence


• Serious offence where bail is privilege and only court can grant
• On being arrested and taken into custody for serious or non-bailable crime, person cannot be ask to be released on bail as matter of right
• Example of Non-Bailable offence
◇ Rape
◇ Murder
◇ Dowry death
◇ Attempt to murder
◇ Kidnapping
