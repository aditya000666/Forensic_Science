Sanctioning Right


• Violation of breach of primary rights
◇ Proceeding to compel a defendant to pay debt to perform contract or to repay money wrongly received
• Sanctioning rights divided into two kinds by reference to purpose of law in creating them
◇ The imposition of monetary penalty upon defendant for wrong which he/she has committed
◇ Provision of pecuniary compensation for plaintiff in respoect of damge which he/she has suffered from defendant wrongholdings
