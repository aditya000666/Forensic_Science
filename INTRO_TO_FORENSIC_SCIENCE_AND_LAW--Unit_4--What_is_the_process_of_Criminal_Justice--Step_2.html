Step 2


• Police officer proceeds to scene of crime and investigates the facts of the case
• Investigation include
◇ Examination of scene of crime
◇ Examination of witness and suspects
◇ Recording of statements
◇ Conducting searches
◇ Seizing property
◇ Collecting fingerprint, footprint and other scientific evidence
◇ Consulting records and making entries in prescribed records like case diary, daily diary, station diary
◇ Making arrests and detentions
◇ Interrogation of accused
