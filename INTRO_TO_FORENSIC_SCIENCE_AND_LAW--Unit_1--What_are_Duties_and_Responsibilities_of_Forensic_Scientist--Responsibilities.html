Responsibilities


• Plays important rule in criminal justice delivery system because there opinion will be factor in determining person involvement in crime or innocence
• First and foremost duty of forensic scientist will be able to apply skills and knowledge for analysis of many evidences by using the principle and techniques of basic science
• Expoert should perform required experiment and observe and collect necessary data
• Expoert should make conclusion based on data that were collected
• He should prepare report without any contradictions and confusions but should be clear
• He should submit the report to court through the law enforcement person who asked for the service
• Along with expoert must send charts, illustrations, photographs and other supporting data
• Written report from expert is accepted in court even without presence of expert himslef
• But if court insists the presence of particular expert then he has to attend the court
• The court may have expression of concern regarding the veracity of scientific evidence
• Such as whether scientific technique can be tested whether the technique has been subjected to peer review whether the technique has error that is permissible whether the scientific technique has been acceptable with scientific commodity
• Expert must be expert to give example from refernce book or from book which data has been matched or confirms
