What Systematic procedure is followed while writing a Report


• Conclusion forms the most important part of report
• It can be definite, indefinite or no conclusion at all
• Information given in report has to be concise but sifficient to be intelligible and prove conclusions satisfactory
• Information given in report should have wordings of interference that are standardized
• The standardization so used increases the clarity and nullifies or avoids the chances of misinterpretation
• The conclusion should be written prominently that it has either written bold or should be captial letters
• Reports should be illustrated with experiment data, photograph whenever necessary
