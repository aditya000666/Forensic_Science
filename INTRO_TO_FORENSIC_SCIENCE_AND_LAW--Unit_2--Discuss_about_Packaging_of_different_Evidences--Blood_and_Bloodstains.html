Blood and Bloodstains


• Blood in liquid pools should be picked up on gauze pad or other clean sterile cotton cloth and allowed to air dry
• Pack gauze pad after drying between clean white paper and send in paper envelope
• Dried stains on cloting, sheet and blankets, send the entire clothing to lab
• Protect the stain with clean white paper and send in paper envelope
• For fresh moist stain on clothing, sheet, blanket allow stain to dry at room temperature
• Do not use packaging device that limites air exchange cause of biological samples gets deteriorated
