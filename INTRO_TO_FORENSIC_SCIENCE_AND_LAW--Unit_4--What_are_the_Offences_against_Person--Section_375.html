Section 375


Rape



• Man is said to commit rape who except in case hereinafter excepted, has sexual intercourse with woman under circumstances falling under any of following description
◇ Against her will
◇ Without consent
◇ With her consent, when her consent has been obtained by putting her or any person in who she is interested in fear of death or of hurt
◇ With her consent, when man knows that he is not her husband and that her consent is given cause she believes that he is another man to whom she believes herself to be lawfully married
◇ With her consent when at time of giving consent by reason of unsoundness of mind or intoxication or administration by him personally or through another of stupefying or unwholesome substance she is unable to understand the nature and consequences of that to which she gives consent
◇ With or without consent, when she is under sixteen years of age
