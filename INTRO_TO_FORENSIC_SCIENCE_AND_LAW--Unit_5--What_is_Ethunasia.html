What is Ethunasia


• Ethunasia also known as Mercy killing
• Easy or painless death or intentional ending of life of person suffereing from an incurable or painful disease at his/her request
• Ethunasia refers to deliberately ending someone's life usually to relieve suffering
• Doctors perform ethunasia on request by perope who have terminal illness

Different Types
• Active
• Passive
• Indirect
• Physical-Assisted Suicide

• Ethunasia legal in Belgium, Canada, Netherland
