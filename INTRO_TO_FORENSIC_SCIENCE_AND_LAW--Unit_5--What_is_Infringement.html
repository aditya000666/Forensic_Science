What is Infringement


• Violation of law or right
• Action of breaking terms of law
• Copyright infringement occurs when copyright owner rights are violated
• Common act of infringement
◇ Making infringing copies for sale or hire or selling or letting them to hire
◇ Permitting any place for performance of works in public where such performance constitutes infringement of copyright
◇ Distributing infringing copies for purpose of trade
◇ Public exhibition of infringing copies by way of trafe
