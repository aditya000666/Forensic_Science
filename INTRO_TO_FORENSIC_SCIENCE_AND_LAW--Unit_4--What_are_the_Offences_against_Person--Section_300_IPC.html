Section 300 IPC


Murder



• Except in case hereafter excepted, culpable homicide is murder if act by which death is caused
• Intention of causing death
• If done with intention of causing such bodily injurity as offender knows to be likely to cause death of person to whom is caused
• If done with intention of casuing bodily injurty to any person and bodily injurity intended to be inflicted is sufficient in ordinary course of nature to cause death
• If person committing the act knows that it is so imminently dangerous that it must cause death and commit such act without excuse for incurring the risk of causing death or such injury
