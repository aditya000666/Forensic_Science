Section 67


Punishment for Publishing or Transmitting Obsene Material in Electronic Form



• Whoever publish or transmit or causes to be published or transmitted in electronic form, any material which is lascivious or appeals to be prurient interest or if its effect is such as to tend to deprave and corrupt persons who are likely, having regard to all relevant circumstances, to read, see or hear the matter container or emboed in it, shall be punished
• First conviction with imprisonment of either description for term which may extend to 3 yrs and with fine which may extend to 5 lakh rupees
• In event of second or subsequent conviction with imprisonment of either description for term which may extend to 5 yrs and also fine which may extend to 10 lakh rupees
