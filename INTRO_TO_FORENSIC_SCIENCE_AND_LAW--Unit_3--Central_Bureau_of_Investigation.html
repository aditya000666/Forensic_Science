Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI)
• India domenstic security agency
• Premier investigative agency in country today
• Dual responsibility to investigate grevious cases and provide leadership and direction in fighting corruption to Police Forces across country
• Formed: 1942 as Special Police Establishment (SPE) to investigate bribary and corruption in transaction with War and Supply Department in India
• Jurisdiction: Government of India
• Headquarters: India
• More of criminal investigation agency
• Resources for Complicated Cases and requested to assist investigation of crimes such as Murder, Kidnapping and Terrorism
• CBI divided into sub-divisions:
◇ Anti-Corruption Division
◇ Special Crime division
◇ Economic Offences division
◇ International Police Cooperation division
◇ Administration Division
◇ Directorate of Prosecution Division
◇ Central Forensic Science Laboratory
