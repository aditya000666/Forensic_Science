Mission and Objectives


• Empower Indian police with information technology and criminal intelligence to enable them to uphold law and protect people
• Provide leadership and excellence in criminal analysis particularly for serious and organised crime
• Store, coordinate and disseminate information on inter-state and internation criminals from and to respective states, national investigating agencies, courts and prosecutors in India without having to infer Police station records
• Funcation as clearing house of information on crime and criminals including operating at National and International levels so as to assist the investigators and other people in linking crime to perpetrator
• Advise Cenral and State governments on matters related to fingerprints and footprints
• Receive from and supply data to penal and correctional agencies for their tasks of rehabilitation of criminals, remand, parole, premature release etc
• Execute and develop computer based systems for Central police organisation and cater their data processing and training needs for computerisation
• Provide training facilities to personnel of Crime Records bureau and evaluate, develop and modernise Crime Record Bureaus
