GEQD Evidence Submission Guidelines
• Developed manual with information for collection, preservation and submission of physical evidence as result of these manual guidelines maximum analytic result can be conclude
• Submitted evidence must be labeled by certain identification mark and include service request form and evidence inventory form or packing slip
• All items to be properly sealed in packing enveloped or in packing container and marked with initials and deeds of ceiling offices
• As Evidence can be altered or tampered during forwarding hence evidence is not properly seized during submission should be immediately returned back to agency without analysis
• Any questions related to collection, preservation or submission of forensic evidence customers are encouraged to contact forensic service laboratory
• For all these tasks, GEQD has logged hours of extensive study and experience in order to become efficient in their field
• GEQD often asked to give official report or even court testimony regarding particular set of documents that studied and assessed
• Handwriting analysis is part of getting Certified as GEQD
• GEQD needs to able to detect forged handwriting and signature along with document authorship
• Erasures and alteration in written document must be determined by document examiner
• GEQD skillful in knowing age of document and determing if there are tampering, addition, ommision and substitution on questioned document
