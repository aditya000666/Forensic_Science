White Collar Crimes


• White collar crime work integrates the analysis of intelligence with investigations of criminal activities such as
◇ public corruption
◇ money laundring
◇ corporate fraud
◇ financial institution fraud
◇ bank fraud
◇ fraud against government
◇ election law violations
◇ mass marketing fraud
◇ health care fraud
• FBI generally focuses on complex investigations often with nexus to organised crime activities that are international, national or regional in scope and where FBI can bring to bear the unique expertise or capabilities that increase the likelihood of successful investigations
• FBI special agents work closely with partner law enforcement and regulatory agencies such as
◇ Security and Exchange commission (SEC)
◇ Internal Revenue Service
◇ US Postal Inspection Service
◇ Commodity Futures Trading Commission
◇ Treasury Department Financial Crimes enforcement network
