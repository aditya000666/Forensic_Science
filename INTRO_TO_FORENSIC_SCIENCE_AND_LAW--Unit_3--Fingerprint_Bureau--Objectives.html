Objectives


• Maintain fingerprint record slips, conduct search of specified categories of Indian and Foreign criminals convicted of offences
• Undertake examination of questioned finger impressions reffered by Central Government department and Govt. of India undertakings
• Impart training in Fingerprinting Science to Police and Non-Police personnel of State Government
• Co-ordinate work of State Fingerprint Bureau and give necessary guidance in all matters related to Fingerprint Science
• Conduct All Indian Board Examination every year for accrediting Fingerprint experts
• Conduct competition in Fingerprint Science at All India Police Duty Meet anually
• Publish Finger Print in India, annual publication of depth study of performance and activities of all Fingerprint Bureau of country
