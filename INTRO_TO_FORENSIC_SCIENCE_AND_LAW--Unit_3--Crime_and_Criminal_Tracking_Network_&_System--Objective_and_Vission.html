Objective and Vission


• Make police functioning citizen friendly and more transparent by automating the functionality of Police stations
• Improve delivery of citizen centric services
• Provide invesitgating officers of civil police with tools and information to facilitate investigation of crime and detection of criminals
• Improve police functioning in various other areas such as Law and Order, Traffic management etc
• Assist senior Police officers in better management of Police force
• Keep track of progress of cases including in courts
• Reduce manual and redundant records keeping
• Under CCTNS, 14000 police stations throughout the country had been proposed to be automated for providing assistance and information for investigation and other purposes
