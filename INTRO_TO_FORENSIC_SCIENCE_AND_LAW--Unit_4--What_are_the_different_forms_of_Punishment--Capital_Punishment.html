Capital Punishment


• Capital means Head or Top of Column
• Means removal of head or Death penalty
• Punishment occupies topmost position among grades of punishment
• Punishment imposed in extreme cases and rarely that too in extremely grave crimes
• Capital punishment can be imposed on criminal who commits a pre-planned and premeditated murder in cold blood
• Offences with sections
◇ High treason: Section 121(IPC)
◇ Abetment of Mutiny: Section 132(IPC)
◇ Murder during or of shooting,explosion,public servant on duty, theft, while resisting lawful arrest, party to murder: Section 302(IPC)
◇ Setting a fire majesty store, ship, person, property
