Marking Evidence


• Evidence labeling is putting a label such as paper bag or envelope with initials and identification number, date and time, case number, evidence number, location and evidence description
• Evidence number must be on each piece of evidence having a unique number
• Evidence description including the type of item, from whom obtained, color of cloting
• Place suspect in controlled area away from victim cloting and crime scene
• Avoid having collector of victim clothing collect the suspect clothing so cross-examination is minimised
