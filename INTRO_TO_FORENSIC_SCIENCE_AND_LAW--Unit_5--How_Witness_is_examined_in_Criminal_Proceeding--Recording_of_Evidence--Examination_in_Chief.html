Examination in Chief


• Examination of Witness by party who calls shall be called Examination in Chief
• First examination after witness has be sworn
• Province of party by whom the witness is called to examine him in chief for the purpose of elicitng from witness all the material facts within his knowledge
• Also known as Direct Examination
• In Criminal Cases, Public Prosecutor does it
• Witness can only give facts of evidence within his knowledge and recollection
