CCIS


• CCIS stands for Crime and Criminal Information System
• Initiative to create crime and criminal related database that can be used to monitor the agencies such as NCRB, SCRB and DCRB
• CIS data is used for publishing online reports such as Missing person report and also used as basis for online query facilities available through NCRB website
• Used by NCRB to publish annual nation-wide Crime report
• CCIS focuses in Crime and Criminal information and does not address other aspects of Police functioning
