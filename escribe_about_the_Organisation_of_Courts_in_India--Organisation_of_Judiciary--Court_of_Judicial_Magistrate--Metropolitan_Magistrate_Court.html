Metropolitan Magistrate Court


• If population area exceed 1 million or more the area is designated as metropolitan area
• District judge is also Metropolitan Session Judge when he is presiding over district court in a city
• Metropolitan Magistrate is first class judicial magistrate under general control of Session Judge and is subordinate of Chief Metropolitan Magistrate
• Metropolitan magistrate pass sentence of imprisonment for term not exceeding 3 yrs or fine not exceeding 10,000 rupees
