Functions of GEQD
• Examination of writings of unknown authors and comparison of writings of known authors with view to fixing identity
• Determination of interpretation, addition, overwriting and use of mechanical and chemical erasure on questioned documents and sometimes need to decipher content of questioned documents
• Examination of torn or serrated edges of paper and comparison with counterparts with counterparts with view to determine if two pieces were parts of sheet
• Examination of couterfeit and forgeries i.e. counterfeit currency, credit cards, passport and other travelling documents
