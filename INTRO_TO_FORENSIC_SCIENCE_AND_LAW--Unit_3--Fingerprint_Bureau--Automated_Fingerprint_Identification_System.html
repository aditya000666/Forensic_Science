Automated Fingerprint Identification System


• Automated Fingerprint Identification System (AFIS) is biometric solution consisting of computer database of fingerprint records which is able to search and compare them to identify known or unknown fingerprints
• First emerged in 1980
• AFIS used for criminal identification have become central to work of police and other law enforcement agencies worldwide
• Indian version of AFIS is called FACTS co-developed NCRB and CMC Ltd. India
• System use Image processing and Pattern recognition technique to capture, encode, store and match fingerprints
• Current version is FACTS 5.0
